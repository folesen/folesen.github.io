HOHO Speech & Laguage Therapy Services

When would my child need to see a SPEECH AND LANGUAGE THERAPIST?

  • Is not speaking as clearly as his/her peers.
  • Is not using as long sentences as other children of his or her age.
  • Seems more reluctant to speak than other children his/her age.
  • Is stammering or stuttering when speaking.
  • Is lacking in social skills when communicating.

About this Service

    This service offers:

  • Pre-meeting phone call to clarify needs and services.
  • A follow up email sharing an information pack describing services, terms and rates and gathering information which will help the therapist plan for the first meeting.
  • Observation and/or assessment in a clinic, school or home setting depending on the needs of the child and parents.
  • A feedback session and report will follow the evaluation period where a management plan is recommended and discussed.
  • A management plan may include scheduling regular therapy sessions, referral onto another service, follow up in an educational setting or putting the student on review.
  • The therapist believes that parents or carers encompass important members of the management team hence parents/carers are encouraged to join and attend sessions.
  • Emails and phone calls are responded to within 24 hours.
